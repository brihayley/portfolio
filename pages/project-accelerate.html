Accelerate Marketing Website

WordPress site

This is my first WordPress site. I received the design for this fictional marketing company through my Skillcrush WordPress class. I went through all the typical install steps, from copying over the client’s “live” site to local, implementing their design request, pushing to a staging site, and going live; to making subsequent edits and adding features, pushing to staging, and going live again. The install process can be a tedious one, but it is thorough and rewarding. This site isn't completely responsive yet, as it's just a demo.
